### Why This Works

- **$79 Starter** undercuts WebPT ($99) and matches TherapyNotes ($79 group) while including AI SOAP notes and AI billing assistant at no extra charge (SimplePractice charges $35/mo extra for AI notes alone).
- **$149 Professional** is the sweet spot for most OT practices. It includes everything — all AI features, unlimited claims, eligibility, telehealth, ERA processing — no add-on fees.
