### AI-Powered Billing & Practice Management for OT Practices

## The Problem

OT practices spend too much time on documentation and billing paperwork. Timed CPT codes require precise unit calculations. Payers have different rules for OT-specific modifiers. Authorization tracking across caseloads is manual. And when claims get denied for coding issues that could have been caught upfront, you lose revenue and spend even more time on appeals. Most practice management tools aren't built with OT billing complexity in mind.

## How TherapyBill AI Saves OTs Time

### AI-Powered OT SOAP Notes
Enter your session details -- interventions used, patient response, functional progress -- and AI generates a complete SOAP note with Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan sections. Understands OT-specific terminology including sensory processing, fine motor, gross motor, ADL performance, and postural control. You review, edit, and sign. The AI does the heavy lifting.

### AI Billing Assistant on Every Page
Have a question about OT billing mid-workflow? Our AI assistant is available on every page. Ask about timed vs. untimed codes, the 8-minute rule, GO modifier requirements, or payer-specific OT policies and get an instant answer. Built-in knowledge of OT CPT codes and common billing scenarios.

### AI Claim Review Before Submission
AI reviews every claim for coding accuracy before it goes out. Catches issues like incorrect unit counts for timed codes, missing modifiers, bundling conflicts between therapeutic exercise and neuromuscular re-education, and payer-specific OT requirements.

### AI Denial Prediction
Analyzes your claims against historical denial patterns and payer-specific rules to flag at-risk claims before submission. Learns from your practice's own denial history to improve accuracy over time.

### AI-Generated Appeal Letters
When a claim is denied, the system generates a payer-specific appeal letter grounded in the clinical documentation, denial reason, and AOTA practice guidelines. Review and send -- instead of spending 30+ minutes drafting from scratch each time.

## Why Fewer OT Claims Get Denied

Claim denials are the single biggest revenue drain for OT practices. TherapyBill AI attacks this problem at every stage:

**Before the session:** Real-time insurance eligibility verification checks patient coverage via direct payer connections (Stedi 270/271 transactions) so you know before treatment begins whether OT coverage is active, what the copay is, and whether authorization is needed. Batch verification lets you check an entire day's caseload at once.

**Before submission:** AI reviews every claim for OT-specific coding accuracy: correct units for timed codes, GO modifier, bundling rules, and payer requirements. Fix issues before submission, not after a 30-day denial cycle.

**After denial:** AI-generated appeal letters citing AOTA guidelines, 60-day deadline tracking, and escalation workflows mean denied claims get appealed quickly and systematically instead of falling through the cracks.

**Over time:** The AI learning service tracks your practice's claim outcomes and denial patterns, generating actionable insights specific to your payers and your coding patterns. The system gets smarter the more you use it.
