Denial Prevention
Why Fewer Claims Get Denied
TherapyBill AI attacks claim denials at every stage of the billing lifecycle.
1

Before the Appointment

Real-time eligibility verification via Stedi (270/271). Batch-check an entire day's schedule. Know coverage, copays, and authorization status before the session.

2

Before Submission

AI reviews every claim for coding accuracy, correct modifiers, and payer-specific requirements. Flags issues like missing info, bundling conflicts, and place-of-service errors.

3

After Denial

AI-generated appeal letters, 60-day deadline tracking, and escalation workflows. Denied claims get appealed quickly instead of falling through the cracks.

4

Over Time

AI learning service tracks outcomes and denial patterns, generating actionable insights specific to your payers. The system gets smarter the more you use it.
